Idea
We wanted to create a to do list app that streamlines the process of setting goals as a team or with friends and working towards getting them done. With "Ta-da" you can create a personal account, create to do lists (with your camera or manually), assign to do list items to team members or friends, discuss to do list items one on one with teammates, and share places to go with others. Together with an easy to use interface and additional fun features, Ta Da will be your new go to task management application.
